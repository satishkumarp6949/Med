Privacy Policy

At Health Partner Medical Store, your privacy is extremely important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following information:

  • Full name, address, phone number, and email
  • Medical prescriptions uploaded through our platform
  • Payment and transaction details
  • Browser cookies and usage data

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your data to:

  • Process your medicine orders and prescriptions
  • Improve our services and user experience
  • Send order updates and promotional content (if opted in)
  • Ensure secure transactions

3. Data Protection

We implement strict security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access or disclosure.

4. Sharing of Information

We do not share your information with third parties except when required by law or to fulfill your order through delivery services.

5. Cookies

We use cookies to improve website functionality. You may choose to disable cookies through your browser settings.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or delete your personal information by contacting us directly.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with the revised date.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this policy, please contact us at: support@healthpartner.com
